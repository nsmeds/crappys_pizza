    Welcome to Crappy's!

    Crappy's Pizza has been delivering steaming hot pies to greater Centralia since 1972. We pride ourselves on fresh ingredients, expert preparation and friendly service.

    Whether you're a cat-skinning carnivore or a tree-sitting vegan, we've got you covered. Check out our classic combinations and signature pies or make your own custom creation with our handy online pizza-builder. Order for dine-in, take-out or delivery with one simple click or feel free to call us at 570-CRAPPYS (570-272-7797).
